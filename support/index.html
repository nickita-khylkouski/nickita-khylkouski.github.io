Ranky Support

Ranky helps you compare photos head to head so you can choose the best one fast.

Contact

Email nickitakhy1@gmail.com for support, bug reports, or deletion requests related to shared boards.

Common questions

  • Ranky does not require an account or login to use the core app.
  • Local photo rankings stay on your device unless you choose to use a shared board.
  • Shared boards use invite-style join codes so other participants can vote on the same set.
  • If a shared board is missing or needs to be removed, include the board title or join code in your email.

Permissions

Ranky may ask for photo library access so you can choose images to rank and save ranked results back into your own library.
